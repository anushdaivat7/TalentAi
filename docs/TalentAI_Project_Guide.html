TalentAI

Intelligent Candidate Discovery & Ranking System  |  Redrob "India Runs" Data & AI Challenge

Project guide, architecture & interview preparation · Role ranked for: Senior AI Engineer (Founding Team)

60-second pitch: TalentAI is an explainable candidate ranking engine. The dataset is full of traps (keyword-stuffers, honeypots, perfect-on-paper-but-wrong-fit profiles), so instead of naive keyword or embedding matching, we score every candidate on five weighted signals, then multiply by a role-fit signal and a trap-penalty that encode what the JD means, not just what it says. It runs on CPU, no network, in under a minute over all 100,000 candidates, and every ranking ships with a grounded, human-readable explanation. Zero honeypots reach our top 100.

2. End-to-End Workflow (JD → ranked output)

  1. Ingest — load candidates.jsonl, validate each record against candidate_schema.json, store in MongoDB (streamed, flat memory).
  2. JD Analysis — parse the JD into a structured profile (required/preferred skills, disqualifiers).
  3. Candidate Understanding — extract skills, experience, projects, education, behavioral signals; build embeddings (offline).
  4. Matching — semantic similarity (cosine) + 5 component scores.
  5. Ranking — apply role-fit & trap modifiers → ordered top 100 (sort by score desc, candidate_id asc).
  6. Explainability — generate grounded reasoning per candidate.
  7. Output — validated submission.csv + dashboard & AI chatbot.

3. The Ranking Formula (the heart of the project)

base  = 0.40 × Skill + 0.25 × Experience + 0.15 × Project
      + 0.10 × Education + 0.10 × Behavioral

final = base × (0.35 + 0.65 × RoleFit) × TrapPenalty      → scaled to 0–100
ComponentWeightWhat it measures
Skill Match40%JD-concept coverage, gated by a "trust" factor (endorsements + months used) so padded skill lists are discounted; blended with semantic similarity.
Experience25%Curve peaking at the ideal 6–8 yrs (5–9 range) + bonus for product companies over IT-services.
Project Relevance15%Did they actually build ranking / search / recsys / retrieval (read from career descriptions).
Education10%Institution tier + relevant field (kept light — JD welcomes plain-language fits).
Behavioral10%Recruiter response rate, recency, open-to-work, interview completion, GitHub activity (availability).
RoleFit (×)mult.Decisive anti-keyword-stuffer signal — title + real career evidence.
TrapPenalty (×)mult.Honeypots → 0; soft penalties for consulting-only, job-hoppers, research-only, CV/speech-without-NLP, inactive.

Key line: RoleFit and TrapPenalty are multipliers, not additions — so even a perfect keyword score can't rescue a fraudulent or wrong-role profile.

7. Key Design Decisions (defend these)

  • Why not an LLM per candidate? No network, CPU only, 5-min budget — 100K LLM calls can't scale; a small ranker over precomputed features can.
  • Why MiniLM over a large model? Quality is good enough and it runs fast on CPU within budget.
  • Why rule-based + semantic hybrid? Rules encode the JD's explicit intent (traps, role-fit); embeddings catch meaning. Together they beat either alone.
  • Why precompute embeddings offline? The spec allows offline pre-computation; the ranking step then loads cached vectors and stays under 5 min.
  • Why multipliers for role-fit & traps? They must be able to override a high keyword score, not just add to it.

8. Likely Judge Questions & Crisp Answers

QuestionAnswer
How do you go beyond keyword matching?Role-fit (title + career evidence) + semantic embeddings + behavioral signals.
How do you handle honeypots?Integrity checks (impossible facts) force them to the bottom — 0 in our top 100.
How do you prevent hallucinated reasoning?Reasoning is built only from the candidate's real profile fields.
How do you meet compute constraints?Embeddings precomputed offline; ranking streams + uses NumPy; ~36s for 100K, no network.
Why these weights?Skill dominates as the core need, but role-fit/traps gate it multiplicatively.
What would you improve?Learning-to-rank (XGBoost), full semantic hybrid at scale, broader honeypot recall.
How does it scale to 200K+?Swap FAISS FlatIP for IVF/HNSW (approx. nearest neighbor); shard embeddings.
